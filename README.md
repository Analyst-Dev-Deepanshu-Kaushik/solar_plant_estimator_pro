# Solar Plant Estimator PRO

A professional, enterprise-grade web application designed for Solar EPC companies and consultants to generate technical and commercial quotations for Solar Power Plants (On-grid, Off-grid, and Hybrid).

* **Persistent Branding:** Upload your company logo once; it stays saved in your browser for all future documents.
* **GST Logic:** Supports both GST "Extra" and "Inclusive" calculations with customizable tax percentages.
* **Editable Terms:** Fully customizable Terms & Conditions section to suit specific project needs.
* **Professional Output:** Generates high-fidelity, A4-sized print-ready PDF documents.
* **Responsive Design:** Fully functional on Desktops, Tablets, and Smartphones with a dedicated Mobile Print option.
* **Activity Log:** Keeps a local history of the last 10 generated estimates for quick reference.
